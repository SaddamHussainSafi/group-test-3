7. Risk Assessment

Technical Risks: Responsive issues across devices → Mitigation: Thorough cross-browser/device testing.
Timeline Risks: Delays due to workload → Mitigation: Weekly milestones and progress reviews.
Collaboration Risks: Miscommunication → Mitigation: Clear task assignment and weekly meetings.
Knowledge Risks: Limited back-end expertise → Mitigation: Focus on front-end first, simplify back-end scope if needed.

8. Communication and Work Plan

Collaboration Tools: GitHub for version control, Google Docs for documentation.
Communication Tools: WhatsApp/Slack for daily updates, Zoom/Google Meet for weekly meetings.
Meeting Schedule: Weekly team check-ins; emergency meetings as needed.
Reporting Structure: Team leader gathers updates and ensures all tasks are aligned with the timeline.
